Our Goal

We plan on making a device to diagnose pneumonia with fairly high accuracy in underprivileged countries. In underprivileged communities/countries, pneumonia is often either not detected or misdiagnosed with malaria or other bronchial diseases. The device we are making should be fairly intuitive to use and allow for an a preliminary diagnosis of pneumonia with a fairly high specificity.
